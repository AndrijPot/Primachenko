Words about the Artist

Ukrainian artist of folk art who worked in the style of naive art. A self-taught artist, she worked in painting, embroidery and ceramics.

Once Pablo Picasso, after visitin Prymachenko's exhibition in Paris at the World Exhibition of 1937, said: "I bow before the artistic miracle of this brilliant Ukrainian."

A street in Kyiv and a small planet are namedafter her.

Honors and awards

Since 1988 - People's Artist of Ukraine.

In 1970, she received the title of Honored Artist of the Ukrainian SSR.

In 1937, at the international exhibition in Paris, she received a gold medal.

In 1966, she received the State Prize of the Ukrainian SSR for the series of paintings "For the joy of people". T. G. Shevchenko.

2009 was recognized by UNESCO as the year of Maria Prymachenko.

Award of the President of Ukraine "National Legend of Ukraine" (August 20, 2022, posthumously)
